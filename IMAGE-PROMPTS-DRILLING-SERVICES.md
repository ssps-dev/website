# Image Prompts — Drilling Services (Sub-Services)

> **Theme Palette**: Warm deep charcoal (#292524), burnished copper/gold (#B8860B), warm off-white (#FAFAF8), stone grays. All images should feel warm, grounded, and premium — golden-hour desert light, dark crude oil textures, amber highlights, dusty earth tones. **Avoid** bright blues, neon colors, or overly saturated stock-photo looks.

> **Style Direction**: Real-life professional photography. Warm color grading (slightly desaturated, amber/golden highlights, deep shadow tones). Cinematic feel. Shot in Oman or Middle Eastern desert/oilfield environments.

> **Dimensions**: 800×450 (16:9 landscape) — used in service cards within discipline sections on the Drilling Services page.

> **File**: `src/components/DrillingServices.tsx` — `disciplines` array
